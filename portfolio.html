Dr.Bob Enofe (LLM, PhD, MBA)


Dr. Bob Enofe (LLM, PhD, MBA): Our Managing Partner and Executive Director, is a seasoned legal and business professional with a wealth of experience in international business law and compliance. His academic credentials include a foundational training in Law from Nigeria, an LLM in International Business Law (with distinction) from the UK, a PhD in Law from Ireland, and a global executive MBA (with distinction) from Brazil.

Dr Enofe also possesses MBA exchange certificates in international trade and international finance from Yale University, SOM, U.S.A and international business strategy from the School of Management, Fudan University, China (completed as part of his MBA degree).

Dr. Enofe has a deep understanding of the law and regulatory frameworks of developing countries, particularly African and Brazilian markets. He has lived and worked internationally across 5 countries. Dr. Enofe has a keen understanding of different cultures, which helps inform the work that we do at Southernbridge Intelligence. As our Managing Partner and Executive Director, Dr. Enofe provides clients with high-quality legal and macroeconomic insights, as well as practical business advice that are well tailored to their unique needs.

Together, our team at Southernbridge Intelligence provides clients with the highest level of service and expertise. Whether you need legal and regulatory advice, macroeconomic insights, or support in developing a business strategy, we are here to help.
